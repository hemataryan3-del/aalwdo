About Us

AALWDO is a development organization operating in Afghanistan dedicated to improving people's living standards, creating alternative livelihoods, and fostering social welfare. We strive to provide sustainable livelihood opportunities and development paths for our citizens.

Our Core Goals & Vision

AALWDO operates to achieve the following fundamental objectives:

  • Providing sustainable alternative livelihoods for farmers and families in remote rural areas.
  • Implementing development projects to elevate health, education, and economic welfare in underprivileged communities.
  • Creating vocational training programs and employment opportunities for youth and women.
  • Delivering emergency humanitarian aid and support to vulnerable citizens during crises.

Our Projects

Key sectors where we design and execute development and supporting projects:

1. Alternative Livelihood Project

Training farmers in high-yield legitimate crop cultivation and distributing modern agricultural tools to empower their socio-economic resilience.

2. Social Welfare & Humanitarian Aid

Providing food packages, essential winter relief items to impoverished families, and digging water wells for safe drinking water access.
